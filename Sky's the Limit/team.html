The faces of Sky's the Limit:

Meet the team behind Sky's the Limit

Sky's the Limit was founded in the year 2045, but traveled backward in time 20 years or more to recruit qualified staff and ceiling fan artisans. We pride ourselves in the diverse array of individuals who make Sky's the Limit their home.

CEO

Our company was founded by a man of vision, with a fascination with ceiling fans. He took Sky's the Limit from a simple start up to the ceiling fan empire it is today. Don't let his small stature and hairy visage fool you - he possesses a shrewd business acumen.

CFO

Hailing from the northern land of Cimmeria, our chief financial officer attained his position by beheading our previous chief financial officer, as is tradition. His name is Conan, and his qualifications speak for themselves. It is from him we learned our guiding mantra of what is best in business: "Crush your enemies, see them driven before you, and hear the lamentation of their shareholders".

COO

Our chief operating officer, Count Dracula, arrived in Canada the last surviving passenger of the ill-fated Demeter. He pulled himself up by his bootstraps, attaining vast wealth and influence using nothing but grit and his inexplicable ability to get people to do what he wants. He tells us we're happy to have him on the team and we're inclined to agree.

CMO

What can be said about our Chief Marketing Officer? Seriously we're asking, because we can't remember anything about them when we're not looking directly at them, and it really hurts our eyes when we do that.
